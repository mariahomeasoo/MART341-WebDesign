Chicken Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten, seasoned with salt
  • 2 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp of chopped ginger
  • 1/2 cup of small diced onion
  • 1 boneless skinless chicken thigh
  • 1/4 cup of carrot, small diced
  • 2 cups of cold jasmine rice, recommended a day old
  • 1/4 cup of frozen peas
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat your wok over the highest heat you can go with your stove.
  1. Prepare vegetables and mix soy sauce and oyster sauce in a small mixing bowl.
  2. Heat a wok over high heat; add butter and let it melt. Pour beaten eggs and scramble. While they are still soft, remove from wok and set aside.
  3. Into the same wok, add oil and swirl to coat. Add garlic, ginger, and onion, stir fry for 30 seconds or until the onions become transparent.
  4. Add the chicken and season with a small pinch of salt and pepper. Cook for about 1 minute or until the chicken is 3/4 way cooked, then stir in carrot and cook for a minute.
  5. Add rice and pour sauce mixture. Keep stirring and tossing until every grain of rice is evenly coated with sauce, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in frozen peas, green onions and scrambled eggs. Stir and cook everyting for about 30 seconds to 1 minute and remove from heat.
  7. Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with sesame seeds.
